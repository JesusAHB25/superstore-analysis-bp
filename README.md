# Superstore Sales Exploration

## Project Overview
This project explores sales, profit, and discount trends in the Superstore dataset. Using SQL for analysis and Power BI for visualization, the goal is to uncover actionable insights to improve business decisions in product pricing, customer segmentation, and regional strategies.

## Objective
- Analyze sales and profit by **category, region, and customer segment**.
- Examine **top-selling products** and their margins.
- Investigate the **relationship between discounts and profit**.
- Provide **actionable recommendations** for business improvements.

## Tools used
- **SQL**
- **SQLite**: Data exploration and SQL queries
- **DB Browser**
- **Power BI**: Visualizations (scatter plot)
- **Notion**: Portfolio documentation and presentation

## Key Insights
- **Categories:** Technology is the most profitable; Furniture has the lowest profit.
- **Regions:** West region leads in sales; South region underperforms.
- **Customer Segments:** Consumers generate most profit; opportunity to grow Corporate and Home Office segments.
- **Products:** Top-selling items are Office Supplies with high volume but lower margins.
